Chocolate Chip Cookies

Indulge in the rich and irresistible flavor of homemade chocolate chip cookies. Soft, chewy, and loaded with gooey chocolate chips, these cookies are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually add to the creamed mixture and mix well.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto the prepared baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
  8. Cool slightly on the baking sheets before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies!
